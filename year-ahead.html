The reading, in a nutshell.

Good Questions

Things you might be wondering.

Should I get this around my birthday?
It's a natural time, since the solar return chart adds another rich layer. But honestly, you can start your year anywhere—what matters is that you have the map.
Does this predict the future?
No, and I don't do predictive astrology in that way. What I do is name the energies present in each window—what they ask of you, what they support—so you can move through your year with awareness instead of reactivity.
Can I combine this with a Soul Blueprint?
Yes—and many people do. The Blueprint gives you the foundational map; the Year Ahead shows you how this year's weather is moving across it. Email me for a combined rate.
What if a hard transit is coming up?
We'll talk about it together. Hard transits aren't bad—they're often where the most meaningful growth lives. I'll help you understand what's being asked of you and how to meet it.
