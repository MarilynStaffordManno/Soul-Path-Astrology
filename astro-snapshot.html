What to Expect

The reading, in a nutshell.

The Astro Snapshot is where most people begin. In one focused hour, we'll explore the three pillars of your chart—your Sun, Moon, and Rising signs—and the soul calling woven through them.

You'll leave with language for parts of yourself you've always sensed but never quite named—and a recording to return to whenever you need to remember.

Good Questions

Things you might be wondering.

Do I need to know anything about astrology beforehand?
Not a thing. I'll meet you exactly where you are. Skeptics, beginners, and long-time chart readers are all equally welcome.
What if I don't know my exact birth time?
Birth time matters most for your Rising sign and houses. If it's unknown, we can still do a meaningful reading focused on Sun and Moon—just let me know when you book.
Is this private?
Completely. Sessions are one-on-one and held in deep confidence. Recordings are for your eyes only.
Can I bring specific questions?
Yes—and I encourage it. The more specific you are about what's alive in your life, the more relevant the reading becomes.
