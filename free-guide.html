What's Inside

A gentle framework for living with the moon.

The moon moves through eight phases roughly every 29 days—and your body, energy, and emotions move with her, whether you notice or not. This guide helps you start noticing, and gives you simple practices for each phase.

🌑

New Moon

Rest, intention-setting, and gentle dreaming. The dark phase of restoration.

🌒

Waxing

Quiet building. Planting seeds, beginning projects, gathering momentum.

🌕

Full Moon

Illumination and release. The phase of seeing clearly and letting go.

🌘

Waning

Integration. Slowing down, reflecting, returning to the dark with gratitude.

Inside the guide you'll find specific rituals, journal prompts, body practices, and energetic invitations for each phase—plus a gentle moon tracker to help you tune in.
