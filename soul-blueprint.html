What to Expect

The reading, in a nutshell.

The Soul Blueprint is for those ready to go beneath the surface. We move past Sun, Moon, and Rising into the deeper architecture of your chart—the aspects, houses, nodal axis, and chart ruler—to map the underlying patterns shaping your life.

This is the reading clients describe as "finally being seen." It's where the questions you've been carrying—about purpose, about why certain themes keep returning—start to make sense as part of a larger design.

You'll leave with a written report you can return to for years. And for 30 days after, you'll have email access to me for follow-up questions as things settle.

Good Questions

Things you might be wondering.

How is this different from the Astro Snapshot?
The Snapshot is a focused intro. The Blueprint goes much deeper—into aspects, the nodal axis, your chart ruler, and the patterns weaving everything together. It also includes a written report and a month of ongoing support.
When will I receive the written report?
Within one week of our live session. I write each one slowly and with care.
What if I need to reschedule?
No problem—just give me 48 hours' notice and we'll find a new time that works.
Is this appropriate during a difficult life transition?
Often, yes. Many people come during transitions—it's when soul-level perspective is most needed. That said, this is not therapy, and I'll always honor what you're ready to explore.
